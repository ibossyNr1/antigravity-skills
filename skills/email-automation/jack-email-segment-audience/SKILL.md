# Email Segment Audience

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When planning email marketing campaigns
- To improve revenue from email marketing

## What This Does

Split audience by demographics, behaviors, and engagement. Segmented campaigns increase revenue. Start with 3-4 key segments.

## Workflow

1. Segment your audience based on demographics, behaviors, and engagement levels.
2. Focus on creating 3-4 key segments to start.
3. Tailor campaigns to each specific segment.
4. Monitor performance and adjust segments as needed.

# Guidance Troubleshoot N8n Make

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- when you need to ask for help debugging an automation workflow

## What This Does

Offers guidelines for effective tech support questions when troubleshooting n8n or Make automations.

## Workflow

1. **Be specific:** Identify the exact part of the automation you're struggling with.
2. **Show what's happening:** Record a short video or share screenshots of your configuration.
3. **Structure your question efficiently:**
   - Start with a one-sentence summary of your issue.
   - List the steps you've already tried.
   - Include error messages.

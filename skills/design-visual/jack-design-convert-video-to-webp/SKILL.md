## What This Does

Convert video files to WebP format for optimized web performance using EZgif.com.

## Workflow

1. Go to EZgif.com.
2. Select the 'Video to WebP' tool.
3. Upload your video file.
4. Adjust settings (if needed).
5. Convert the video to WebP format.
6. Download the WebP file for use on your website.

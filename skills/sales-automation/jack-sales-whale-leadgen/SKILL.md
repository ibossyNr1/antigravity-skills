# Sales Whale Leadgen

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When targeting high-paying clients
- When standard lead generation methods are not effective

## What This Does

Procedure to attract high-paying clients through personalized outreach using Loom videos and potentially physical mail.

## Workflow

1. **Identify Whales:** Target high-paying clients in your niche.
2. **Personalized Outreach:** Create personalized Loom videos explaining how you can solve their specific problems.
3. **Value Proposition:** Clearly state your value proposition in one line: "Personalised why in 1 liner to value X in Y Days".
4. **Unsaturated Channels:** Consider using physical mail or other less common channels to stand out.
5. **LinkedIn/Email/Telephony:** Use a multi-channel approach using LinkedIn, email, and telephone.

**Required tools/platforms:**
- Loom

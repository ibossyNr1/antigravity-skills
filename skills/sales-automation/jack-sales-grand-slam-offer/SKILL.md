# Sales Grand Slam Offer

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When creating a new offer
- When current offers are not effective

## What This Does

Framework for building an irresistible 'Grand Slam Offer' by focusing on outcome, hurdles, solutions, delivery, and refinement.

## Workflow

1. **Outcome:** Clearly define the dream outcome for your customer.
2. **Hurdles:** Identify the obstacles preventing them from achieving that outcome.
3. **Solutions:** Present your services as the solution to overcome these hurdles.
4. **Delivery:** Detail how you will deliver the solution.
5. **Refine:** Continuously improve the offer based on feedback and results.

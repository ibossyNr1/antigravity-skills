## What This Does

Provides a structured approach for troubleshooting voice agent setups, including specifying the issue, providing visual evidence, and outlining steps tried.

## Workflow

To effectively troubleshoot issues:
1. **Be specific:** Identify the exact part of the setup causing problems (e.g., voice agent configuration, webhook connection).
2. **Provide visual evidence:** Use Loom videos or screenshots showing the configuration and error messages.
3. **Structure your question:** Summarize the issue, list steps already tried, and include error messages.

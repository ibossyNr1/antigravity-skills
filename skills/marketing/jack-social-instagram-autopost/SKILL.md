# Social Instagram Autopost

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When you want to automate the process of posting photos to your Instagram for Business account.

## What This Does

Automatically create photo posts on Instagram for Business using Make.com.

## Workflow

1. Add the 'Instagram for Business > Create a Photo Post' module in Make.com.
2. Connect your Instagram account and select the target page.
3. Set the photo URL and caption inputs using data from previous modules.

**Required tools/platforms:**

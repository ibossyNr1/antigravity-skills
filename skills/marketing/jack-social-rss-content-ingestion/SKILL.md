# Social Rss Content Ingestion

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When you want to automatically pull content from blogs or newsletters.

## What This Does

Fetch content from RSS feeds of chosen topics or newsletters.

## Workflow

1. Create an account at rss.app.
2. Search for relevant RSS feeds for your niche.
3. Configure filters and keywords.
4. Copy the XML URL of the feed.

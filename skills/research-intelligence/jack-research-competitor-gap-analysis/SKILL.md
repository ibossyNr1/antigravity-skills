# Research Competitor Gap Analysis

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- when planning features for a SaaS product
- when differentiating a SaaS offering

## What This Does

Leverage review sites like G2 and Capterra to identify gaps in competitor offerings by filtering for 1-star reviews and 'Cons'.

## Workflow

1. Identify major competitors in your desired niche.
2. Search for these competitors on G2 and Capterra.
3. Filter reviews by '1-star' or 'Cons'.
4. Analyze the negative feedback to identify what the competitor is missing.
5. Use identified gaps to inform feature development for your SaaS.

# Leadgen Lead Magnet Testing

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When you want to improve the performance of your lead magnets.
- When conversion rates are low

## What This Does

Test lead magnets for effectiveness to optimize lead generation and conversion rates.

## Workflow

1. Define clear goals for the lead magnet.
2. Establish metrics for success (e.g., download rate, conversion to trial, qualified lead).
3. A/B test different lead magnet types and content variations.
4. Track results and analyze data to identify best performers.
5. Iterate on successful lead magnets to further improve metrics.
